# Dashboard Layout Redesign and Custom Animations

## General Layout & Whitespace Refinements
- **Compact Padding**: Reduced the overall vertical whitespace between the primary container and the Navbar (`md:py-10` → `md:py-4`).
- **Title Gap**: Tightened the margin below the main dashboard title (`mb-2` → `mb-1`).
- **Section Spacing**: Decreased the margin gaps beneath the Impact Scorecards and the Contribution Heatmap (`mb-6` → `mb-4`) to create a tighter, more cohesive visual hierarchy.

## Impact Scorecard Sizing
- **Card Height Optimization**: Greatly reduced the vertical height of the two impact scorecards ("Total Stars" and "Total Forks").
- **Internal Padding**: Swapped uniform padding (`p-6`) for tightened vertical padding (`py-3 px-5`).
- **Typography & Iconography**: Shrunk the primary statistic font size (`text-4xl` → `text-3xl`) and scaled down the corresponding SVG icons and their circular wrappers for a sleeker look.

## Grid Realignment (Left & Right Columns)
- **Vertical Chart Grouping**: Refactored the charts section into two distinct flex columns. 
- **Left Column**: Stacked the `Language Distribution` Pie Chart and `Repository Topics` Word Cloud vertically underneath each other.
- **Right Column**: Maintained the two bar charts (`Top Starred` and `Most Forked`).
- **Pie Chart Scaling**: Reduced the inner (`90`) and outer (`130`) radii of the Language Distribution donut chart to neatly fit its new half-width column. Shifted its `cy` anchor to `45%` and adjusted its bottom margins to allocate plenty of clean, uncluttered space for the interactive chart legend at the bottom.

## Repository Topics UI & Word Cloud Animations
- **White Theme Override**: Restructured the Repository Topics card completely to use a clean, solid white background (`bg-white`) with dark text (`text-gray-900`), removing the previous dark gray wrapper borders.
- **Palette Update**: Updated `wordCloudColors` to a rich, dark, high-contrast palette (crimson, emerald, navy blue, deep purple) guaranteeing crisp legibility against the white canvas.
- **Framer Motion & CSS Integration**: Imported `framer-motion` and injected a custom `<style>` block directly into the dashboard.
- **True Word Vibration**: Implemented a `wordFloat` CSS keyframe animation utilizing `opacity` flickering and shifting `drop-shadows` rather than `transform: scale`, ensuring D3's inline SVG coordinates aren't disrupted.
- **Staggered Delays**: Applied the animation to the word cloud's `<text>` tags using `nth-child` targeting, generating independent vibration speeds and delays so each individual topic pulses organically.
